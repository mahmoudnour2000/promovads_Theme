About OpenAI

OpenAI is an AI research and deployment company. Our mission is to ensure that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity. We build safe and beneficial AI systems and make them widely available.

The Role

We are looking for a Senior AI Engineer to join our LLM Infrastructure team. In this role, you will be responsible for building and scaling the systems that power some of the world's most widely used AI products, including ChatGPT, GPT-5, and the OpenAI API.

You will work closely with research teams to productionize new model capabilities, optimize inference pipelines for cost and latency, and design fault-tolerant distributed systems that handle millions of requests per second.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and implement high-performance inference pipelines for large-scale language models
  • Optimize model serving for latency, throughput, and cost efficiency
  • Build distributed systems that scale to millions of concurrent users
  • Collaborate with research teams to bring new model capabilities to production
  • Drive architectural decisions for next-generation infrastructure
  • Mentor junior engineers and contribute to team culture

Required Qualifications

  • 5+ years of experience in software engineering with focus on ML systems
  • Strong proficiency in Python and at least one compiled language (C++, Rust, Go)
  • Experience with distributed systems design and implementation
  • Deep understanding of ML model architectures, especially Transformers
  • Experience with GPU programming (CUDA, Triton, or similar)
  • BS/MS/PhD in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or equivalent

Benefits

  • 💰 Competitive salary ($200K – $350K) + equity + bonus
  • 🏥 Full health, dental, and vision insurance for you and dependents

  • 🧠 Work on the most exciting AI problems in existence
  • 🌴 Unlimited PTO + 20 company holidays

OpenAI values concrete examples of systems you've built at scale. Include GitHub links, papers, or case studies that demonstrate your impact.
