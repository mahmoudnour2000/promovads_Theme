Mbappe's Hat-Trick Shatters Barcelona: El Clasico Match Report

Kylian Mbappe was simply unplayable as Real Madrid dismantled their rivals in a stunning El Clasico performance, moving 7 points clear at the La Liga summit.

Alex Roberts
Football Correspondent
June 15, 2026 5 min read 284,000 views

Three goals, three different styles, one absolutely dominant performance. Kylian Mbappe's masterclass at the Bernabéu will go down as one of the greatest individual El Clasico performances in history as Real Madrid demolished Barcelona 3-1.

The first goal came from the penalty spot after Koundé's needless handball, but the second was a moment of pure genius — Mbappe receiving the ball 22 yards out and curling a right-footed effort into Ter Stegen's top corner. Even the travelling Barcelona fans applauded.

"He is a different category of player. What Mbappe did tonight cannot be explained with football terms alone." — Carlo Ancelotti, Real Madrid Manager

Barcelona briefly threatened through Lewandowski's header, but any thoughts of a comeback were extinguished by Mbappe's third — a devastating counter-attack in the 87th minute that sealed a famous victory.
